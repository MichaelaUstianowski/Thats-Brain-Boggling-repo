Here is a glossary of technical terms used in this website and what they mean.

  • Cerebral Cortex - The outermost layer of the brain.
  • Hemipsheres - Halves of the brain. The brain has a left and a right hemisphere.
  • Neurons - Also known as nerve cells, neurons send and recieve chemical and electrical signals from the brain.
