Here is a glossary of technical terms used in this website and what they mean.

  • Cerebral Cortex - The outermost layer of the brain.
  • Cognitive Planning - A mental process that allows us to choose the correct actions to achieve a goal, decide the correct sequence of actions, allocate tasks to different parts of the brain,
  • Corpus Collosum - A bunch of nerve fibres which connect the right and left hemispheres of the brain.
  • Hemispheres - Halves of the brain. The brain has a left and a right hemisphere.
  • Lesions - Areas of the brain which are injured.
  • Neurons - Also known as nerve cells, neurons send and receive chemical and electrical signals from the brain.
  • PET - Acronym for Positron Emission Tomography, it is a type of brain scan technique.
  • Stimuli - Things or events that can trigger a specific reaction.
