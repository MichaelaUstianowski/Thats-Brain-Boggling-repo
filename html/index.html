The Brain

The brain is made of cells called neurons which send electrical messages all over the brain. The cerebral cortex has two hemispheres and four areas which are responsible for different processes. Hover over the buttons by the brain to find out where each area is.

The left and right hemispheres

The hemispheres of the brain are associated with different functions. For example, the left side is more associated with language, logic and reasoning but the right is more associated with creativity. Furthermore, the left side of the brain controls the actions of the right side of the body, whereas the right side of the brain controls the movements of your left side of the body. Despite the idea of being right or left brained, we use both halves in connection. The hemispheres are connected by a colelction nerve fibers called the Corpus Collosum which allows messages to be passed to each hemipshere. This video is about a patient who had had his Corpus Collosum cut, due to his severe epilepsy. It provides a quite interesting insight into how each hemisphere functions separately.
